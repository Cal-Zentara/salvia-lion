The Process

Simple, gentle, real

01

Before Your Session

Salvia meditates, grounds herself, and calls in her guides to prepare. She asks for help on your behalf before you even arrive — so the space is already held for you.

02

During Your Session

She calls in your guides so you're all working together. She notes every feeling, sensation, thought, and visual that comes up — nothing is missed, nothing is rushed.

03

After Your Session

Salvia follows up to ask how you feel and debriefs with you. The cards pulled are summarized and sent to you by email, along with information on the chakras that came up during your session.
