How to use the hooks

Surprise stops a stranger. Recognition makes them save.

The hook is the cover slide on a carousel, the first 2 seconds of a Reel, or the first line of a caption. Its only job is to stop the scroll.

So the hook opens with a jolt, and the body pays it off with the "that's me" feeling. Never lead with the soft, pretty recognition line. Save that for slide 2 or 3.

Three moves do all the work:

  • Numbered signs ("3 signs...") — best for teaching carousels. Scannable, gets saved most.
  • Counterintuitive claim ("it's not X, it's Y") — surprise. Best for myth-busting topics.
  • Curiosity question or "nobody talks about..." — opens a loop they have to close. Best for connect posts.

By day:

  • Monday (Teach) — a numbered "signs" hook or a myth-bust. Body teaches.
  • Wednesday (Connect) — a question or a process pull ("come pack an order with me"). Body invites a reply.
  • Friday (Sell / Prove) — a specific reframe or a story curiosity ("she didn't even like baths"). Body makes the soft offer.

Instagram SEO — keywords for every caption

Instagram reads your words now, not your hashtags.

IG decides who to show a post to by reading the caption, the on-screen slide text, and what's said aloud in a Reel. Put 2 to 3 of these in the first line of every caption, and a keyword phrase on the cover slide.

Who she's for: empath · highly sensitive person · HSP · sensitive soul · intuitive · lightworker

What they feel: absorb other people's energy · energetic overwhelm · drained after crowds · overstimulated · energy clearing · energetic protection · nervous system reset · boundaries for empaths

The product / the fix: Reiki charged · Reiki bath · bath salts ritual · spiritual bath · energy cleansing bath · grounding · aura cleansing · self care ritual

Example first line: "Reiki charged bath salts for highly sensitive empaths who absorb everyone's energy."

  • Stay in her voice. Only use the words that actually sound like her. Never force a keyword or overclaim.
  • The cover slide text is indexed too, so a hook that carries a keyword doubles as SEO ("3 signs the exhaustion you feel isn't even yours" already says exhaustion + signs).
  • No dashes as punctuation, same as always.

Source: Meta AI, June 3 2026, aligned with current IG SEO guidance.
