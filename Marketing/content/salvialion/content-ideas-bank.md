## IDEA 1 — "The hidden cost of always putting others first"
Source: @mindset.therapy style (screenshotted June 6 2026). People-pleaser / boundaries theme.

Why it fits Salvia: her audience are empaths and givers who pour out until they're drained. This is the emotional root of why they need energy clearing. Strong save-and-share material.

The full 8 beats (to adapt, not copy):
1. Cover: The hidden cost of always putting others first
2. You forget what your own needs even feel like (your own voice became the quietest in your life)
3. You say yes when your body is screaming no (people-pleasing is fear wearing a mask)
4. You attract people who only know how to take (give without limits, you train people to take without gratitude)
5. You feel guilty for resting (your brain says worth comes from being useful, so you never relax, you just wait until you're needed again)
6. You are everyone's therapist but nobody asks about your day (you hold space for everyone, but when it's your turn to fall apart the room goes silent)
7. You lose yourself so slowly you don't even notice (one small sacrifice at a time, until you don't recognize the person in the mirror)
8. THE TURN: Putting yourself first is not selfish, it's the only way to actually show up for others (you can't pour from a cup that's been empty for years)

Salvia angle for the close: this is what absorbing everyone's energy does to you. Reiki and the bath ritual are how you start choosing yourself again. End on slide 8's "putting yourself first is not selfish" turn, then a soft invite. No hard sell.
