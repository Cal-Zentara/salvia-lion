**Why each day does what it does:**
- **Monday teaches** because helpful posts get *saved*, and saves are what the algorithm uses to show her to new people. Teaching is how a small account grows. (Mosseri 2025; PostNitro 2025.)
- **Wednesday connects** because the middle of the week is for trust. Making someone feel seen, showing her hands charging the bags, or asking a question gets *replies and shares* — the strongest signal on Instagram in 2026. (Mosseri 2025.)
- **Friday sells or proves** because by the end of the week she's earned the right to ask. Selling stays about 1 in 5 posts so it never feels pushy. A real testimonial does the selling for her. (60/20/20 value-to-sell mix, nDash 2025.)

The whole point: teach to get found, connect to build trust, then sell. Never sell to a stranger who doesn't trust her yet.

## The system (why it's built this way)

Research finding (Mosseri 2025, Brandwatch 2025, Craftybase 2026): small accounts grow on **teaching content** (earns saves) and **process video** (people love watching things get made), NOT on selling. Selling stays about 1 in 5 posts. So every week follows the same three-slot rhythm:

- **MONDAY = TEACH** — an educational carousel. Earns saves, which the algorithm rewards with reach. This is the growth engine.
- **WEDNESDAY = CONNECT** — rotates between making someone feel seen, a behind-the-scenes video of her charging the bags, or a question that gets replies. Builds the relationship and the DMs.
- **FRIDAY = SELL or PROVE** — rotates between a gentle offer and a real testimonial or her story.

Roughly 70% value, 11% community, 18% selling. That's the research-backed mix.
