### 5. What's really going on in the buyer's head (consumer psychology)

People decide with emotion, then justify with logic. We don't have to guess — the buyer research and her own client notes tell us what they feel:

- **Being seen ("finally, someone gets it") — the strongest pull.** They've felt drained for years with no name for it. "You're not imagining it" lands harder than any product claim.
- **Relief ("there's a simple thing I can do at home").** They don't want a lifestyle. They want to put down what they're carrying before they walk into their sanctuary.
- **Safety / trust ("she's real, not a brand").** Her story, her credential (certified Reiki Master), and her restraint are the proof. She never oversells — that *is* the trust signal.
- **Specific outcomes beat vague ones.** Not "relax" — "clear, grounded, protected," "feel like yourself again." The buyer research says scent is the #1 trigger, then instructions, then the maker's story.

Stop selling salt. Sell the relief of putting down what isn't yours. Real outcomes and her real story beat any "Shop Now."

### 7. How to write any post — AIDA (in her voice)

The 4 steps every post moves through, in order. Her voice: quiet, warm, empathy first, no urgency, no dashes as punctuation.

- **Attention** (stop the scroll, open with a memory or an observation, never a pitch): "You leave work exhausted but it's not from your workload."
- **Interest** (now she cares): "It's from absorbing everyone else's energy all day. I used to experience this without knowing what it was."
- **Desire** (now she wants it, carry it with a real, understated outcome): "One Reiki session changed everything for me. These bath salts exist because of that. Most people say they feel relaxed or a little sleepy."
- **Action** (tell her exactly what to do, soft and non-pushy): "There's a reason you were led here. DM me to order." *(invitation, never "shop now")*

Earn the close, don't skip to it. **Her story is the spine, the real outcome is the proof, the $10 is the kicker.** Recognition hooks stop the scroll up top, but every post still lands on her realness as the reason to trust.
